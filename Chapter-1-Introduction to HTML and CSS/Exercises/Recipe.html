Omelette

Quick. Easy. Delicious.

Description

An Omelette is usually a battered eggs that is then fried on a pan with oil or butter

They are then folded around other combination of ingredients

ingredients

  • Any amound of eggs
  • Butter/Oil
  • Chives
  • Onions
  • Tomato
  • Salt
  • Pepper

    • Method

    1. Bring Pan to low heat
    2. Crack the desired amount of eggs and whisk till mixed
    3. Dd Oil or Butter to the pan
    4. Gently pour eggs
    5. Wait till one side is cook before flipping
    6. Lay it flat and put desired filling in the center
    7. Fold it. And optinally add chive as toppings and serve
